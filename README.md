# Ultimate WGT Pro AI

**Elite-level WGT Golf shot simulator and AI assistant** for competitive players.  
This app provides **shot calculations, club recommendations, 3D trajectory visualization, AR overlays, and analytics** for WGT Golf, powered by AI and physics simulation.

---

## Features

- **AI Screenshot Analyzer** – Detect distance, green slope, wind, and pin position from uploaded screenshots.
- **Shot Calculator** – Recommends optimal club, aim, and spin based on distance, wind, and slope.
- **Putt Calculator** – Calculates aim adjustment and stroke power for precise putting.
- **3D Trajectory Visualizer** – Simulates ball flight, landing, and rollout on green.
- **AR Overlay** – Highlights pins, wind direction, and predicted shot path.
- **Club Bag** – Displays all clubs, max distance, and accuracy; allows manual selection.
- **Analytics Dashboard** – Tracks shot history, accuracy, and performance trends.
- **Cross-Platform** – Works on Web, Desktop (Electron), Android, and iOS (Capacitor).
- **Sample Screenshots & Icons** – Pre-loaded for immediate testing.
